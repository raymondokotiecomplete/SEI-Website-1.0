SE Innovations Financial Platform

As part of our vision, our innovations operate a unique financial platform tailored to our growth, goals, and objectives. Our goal is to empower the youth with money, teach them to grow it, and become independent.

This runs simultaneously with all our skills and training programs. It's simple: To participate in our skill acquisition programs, you are required to pay a registration fee of ₦3,000.
