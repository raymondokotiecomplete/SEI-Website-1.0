SE Innovations Financial Platform

As part of our vision, SE Innovations operates a unique financial platform tailored to our growth, goals, and objectives. Our goal is to empower youths with money, teach them how to grow it, and become independent.

This runs simultaneously with all our skill and training programs. It's simple: to participate, pay a registration fee of ₦3,000 and gain access to various benefits and tools.

FAQs

Q: How do I pay the registration fee?

A: You can pay via bank transfer, mobile money, or cash payment during registration.

Q: Can I join multiple programmes with one registration?

A: Yes, once registered, you can participate in any of our programs.

Q: Is there any financial support available?

A: Occasionally, we offer sponsorships. Contact us to inquire about current opportunities.
